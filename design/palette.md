# ICE Visual Palette

## Core Colors

- Ice Blue: `#8FB9FF`
- Ice Cyan: `#6EE7F9`
- Ice Violet: `#7A7CFF`

## Backgrounds

- Primary: `#0B0F14`
- Secondary: `#111827`
- Surface: `#161B22`

## Text

- Primary: `#E6EDF3`
- Secondary: `#9AA4AF`
- Muted: `#6B7280`

## Usage Rules

- No warm colors
- No white backgrounds
- No saturated gradients
- Accents only, never dominant
